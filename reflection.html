Group Reflection

Overall our group was far more organised in comparison to the last assignment. Firstly, since to -do lists were something that helped us in delegating tasks in the last assignment, we decided to continue using them however we refined them. Instead of only listing tasks we assigned names to each of the tasks within the list of when we expected the task to be completed by and a check box which was to be marked when a task was completed by a member. This helped to not only ensure that tasks were reasonably evenly distributed between group members so that tasks were not left to last minute to complete therefore lowering the level of stress we felt, but it also allowed others within the group to see what stage everyone in the group was at and whether and where we were up to in the assignment. Since scheduling group calls on Microsoft teams was difficult due to everyone's busy schedules and even meant some members were not even able to join the call, we were still able to ensure all members were on the same page and understood their individual tasks as well as what the group had discussed/ decided during the meetings through checking the to do list. Although our communication was better in this assignment compared to the last, we still had issues. As mentioned above, some members were unable to join the scheduled group calls and therefore made it difficult to delegate tasks evenly and discuss steps that should be taken by the group moving forward since we were unable to receive all members feedback and therefore meant that some decisions were forced to be made by others due to time constraints. As a group we were unhappy with our performance in the last assignment and saw how we had the potential to do a lot better, therefore the group's motivation and organisation for assignment 3 was very surprising. Even though we got a later start to this project idea, we were able to establish clear timelines to ensure nothing was left to the last minute. We learnt how motivated our group becomes where we know we have potential to do better and how we all can actually work very well together if we have strong communication to establish clear guidelines. Overall it was a really enjoyable experience working together as a group and being able to utilise all team members' strength to create this assignment.
